Frequently Asked Questions

Is Notewise really free? expand_more

Yes. All academic notes, previous year questions, and syllabus mappings are 100% free for BCA students. We believe education should be accessible to everyone.

Can I contribute my own notes? expand_more

Currently, we operate on a strict curation model. A team of academic reviewers vets all materials before they are published to maintain quality. However, you can use the contact form below to suggest potential additions to the archive.

How frequently are PYQs updated? expand_more

We update our Previous Year Questions database at the end of every semester (typically January and July).
